About:

This is a website to celebrate different artists work that exists on the web. You can find a few selected audio clips in the red list. It's a mix of music and writings. The audio is paired with a digital three-dimensional space. The scene is built in Sketchup using existing models that have been shared by other users in their 3D-warehouse. By clicking through the scenes you can walk through your own visual narrative paired with the audio on this website.

If you would like to create your own three-dimensional space you are very welcome to download my model to use as a starting point. The links to the models can be found in the bottom right box when you have chosen an audio piece.

Instructions:

Choose an audio file in the list.
Click on the upper right box to enlarge the 3D file.
Enlarge the menu by clicking the small arrow on the far right.
Click the scenes menu (play icon).
Start the audio file.
Click through the scenes as the audio plays.
